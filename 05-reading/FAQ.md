# FAQ

## Is this a political system?

No. It does not define policies or outcomes. It defines constraints on authority.

---

## Who is this for?

Anyone designing, evaluating, or interacting with systems of institutional power.

---

## Does this require adoption by governments?

No. It can be analyzed, adapted, or applied in parts or in full.

---

## Why is it written this way?

The system separates:

- human-readable explanations  
- binding structural rules  

This prevents interpretation from expanding authority.

---

## What makes this different?

It assumes failure and designs for containment.

Most systems assume correct behavior and attempt to correct mistakes after the fact.

---

## How should this be read?

Start with:

- WHAT-THIS-IS.md  
- HOW-TO-READ-THIS.md  

Then proceed to the core documents.

---

## Is this complete?

No system is final. This is a structured attempt to define enforceable limits on power.
